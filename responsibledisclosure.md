2. Scope

This policy applies to:

Kinetics Plus LLC public-facing websites

Systems, services, and applications owned or operated by Kinetics Plus LLC

Microsoft 365 services used internally (SharePoint, Teams, OneDrive, etc.)

Any additional systems listed in future updates to this policy

Out-of-Scope Systems

Testing is not permitted on:

Third-party systems not owned by Kinetics Plus LLC

Customer or vendor infrastructure

Systems containing sensitive PII or confidential data

Physical facilities or devices

Any system unless explicitly listed as in-scope

When in doubt, email us before testing.

3. Safe Harbor

We support good-faith security research.
Researchers who adhere to this policy:

Are authorized to perform limited, non-intrusive testing

Will not be subject to legal action by Kinetics Plus LLC

Will not have their research reported to law enforcement

Will have accidental access treated as a good-faith error if reported immediately

Safe Harbor applies only if the researcher complies with all conditions in this policy.

Safe Harbor Requires:

No service disruption

No exfiltration, modification, or deletion of data

No privacy violations

No social engineering

Immediate reporting of unintended access

4. Prohibited Activities

Researchers must not:

Access or attempt to access customer data

Perform Denial-of-Service (DoS or DDoS) attacks

Deploy malware, ransomware, or exploit kits

Attempt social engineering (phishing, vishing, impersonation)

Perform brute-force attacks

Conduct physical security testing

Use automated scanners in a way that degrades system performance

6. Our Response Commitments

Kinetics Plus LLC will:

Acknowledge your report within 5 business days

You will receive a confirmation of receipt.

Validate the issue within 15 business days

We may request additional information if needed.

Prioritize remediation based on severity

Critical and high-risk vulnerabilities receive expedited treatment.

Maintain communication

We will provide updates throughout the investigation and remediation process.

Coordinate public disclosure

We request a standard 90-day disclosure window, unless the vulnerability is fixed sooner and early disclosure is mutually agreed upon.

8. Legal

By participating in this program, you agree:

To follow all applicable laws

To conduct research only in good faith

To comply fully with all required rules of engagement

Safe Harbor may not apply if a researcher acts maliciously, recklessly, or outside this policy.

Stored securely

Not shared externally without permission
