**Kinetics Plus LLC**  
**Effective Date:** 10/01/2025

---

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability affecting Kinetics Plus LLC systems, services, or applications, we encourage you to report it to us as quickly as possible.

Include the following details, when possible:

We ask that you **do not publicly disclose** the vulnerability until we have confirmed and resolved the issue under coordinated disclosure timelines.

---

## Response Process

Kinetics Plus LLC commits to the following:

### **1. Acknowledgment**
We will acknowledge receipt of your report within **5 business days**.

### **2. Validation**
We will investigate and validate the vulnerability within **15 business days**, depending on severity.

### **3. Remediation**
We prioritize remediation based on severity and impact. Critical issues may be addressed immediately.

### **4. Ongoing Communication**
We will keep you informed throughout the validation and remediation process.

### **5. Coordinated Disclosure**
We request a **90-day disclosure window** unless a fix is deployed sooner and early disclosure is mutually agreed upon.

---

## Scope

In-scope systems include:

- Public-facing websites owned or operated by Kinetics Plus LLC  
- Internal systems and services owned by Kinetics Plus LLC  
- Microsoft 365 services used by our organization (SharePoint, Teams, OneDrive, etc.)

### Out-of-scope systems include:

- Third-party platforms not owned by Kinetics Plus LLC  
- Customer or vendor environments  
- Physical security testing  
- Social engineering attempts  
- Denial-of-Service (DoS/DDoS) attacks  
- Any testing that may impact system availability or data integrity

If you are unsure whether something is in scope, please contact us before testing.

---

## Safe Harbor

We support good-faith security research.

If you adhere to this policy:

- Your testing is **authorized**  
- We will **not** seek legal action against you  
- Your findings will be treated respectfully and confidentially  
- Accidental access to sensitive data, if immediately reported, will be treated as good-faith research

Safe Harbor applies **only if you comply fully** with this policy.
